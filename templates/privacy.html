1. Information We Collect

We collect information that you participate in providing to us and information automatically collected through the use of our services.

  • Personal Data: Emails, usernames, names, and passwords for account creation.
  • Usage Data: How you interact with our platform, pages visited, agent usage parameters, and time spent.

2. How We Use Information

We use your information to operate, maintain, and improve our platform. Specific uses include:

  • Creating, managing, and authenticating your account and profile.

  • Sending vital system updates, security alerts, and administrative messages.

3. Information Sharing

Protecting user data is critical. We do not share your personal information with third parties except as necessary to provide our services intrinsically, comply with the law, or protect our operational rights.

4. Data Security

We implement robust and reasonable organizational, technical, and administrative measures to protect personal information under our control. However, please remember that no data transmission over the Internet can be guaranteed to be absolutely 100% secure.
